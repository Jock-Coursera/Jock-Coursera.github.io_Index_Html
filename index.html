Being Ready

Ready to act, in case of emergency* and/or in planning for the future - to ensure you have everything important in your life documented, copied and stored where it is safe for you to retrieve or for someone you nominate (if it cant be you!).

These are the items you might want to put together and place in a secure "Go Bag" but that you should also have copies of and descriptions of which can be kept in a secure "offsite" location for access should something go astray with the originals.

Make a checklist

Things like

  • Property title(s)
  • Will(s)
  • Mortgage documents, rental agreements
  • Insurance Policies

Think about where to keep your valuables

Once you have done all of the legwork (details to come), you will have a clear plan and be ready for any eventuality. GOOD LUCK
